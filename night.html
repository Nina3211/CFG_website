Night Plans

Here are some ideas for things to do at night in Glasgow.

Tingle

This small bar is the defininition of cheap and cheerful.

It has £3.50 cocktails and £2.50 shots

They also have free event hire, for 100+ guests

Plan a trip

The Corinthian Club

If you are feeling more upmarket, The Corinthian Club has over five floors of gorgeously designed bars,

cafes and even a club.

Their Late Night Teller's Bar is open until 3am on the weekends, with a house/disco DJ.

Plan a trip

Boteco do Brasil

Boteco do Brasil brings Latina flavour all the way to Glasgow-

at an affordable price! There is live music nearly everyday

And an open mic every wednesday, with £4.50 cocktails Sunday-Wednesday!

Plan a trip
