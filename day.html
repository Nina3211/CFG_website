Here are some ideas for things to do during the day in Glasgow.

The University of Glasgow

The University of Gasgow is in the heart of the West End.

It is a beautiful castle, often compared to Hogwarts, and is open for visits throughout the year.

You can visit the Gift Shop in the main building for some Glasgow University merchandise!

Kelvingrove Park and Museum

Kelvingrove Museum is an educational and interesting museum to visit on a day out.

It has sections dedicated to science, art and history.

You could even visit the park afterwards for a late afternoon picnic, sit on a hill and have a beautiful view of the city below.

Plan a trip

The Science Centre

The Science Centre is located on the south bank of the River Clyde.

It's full of exciting items and exhibitions, including a planetarium.

There's also a cinema on site so you can end the day with a film!
