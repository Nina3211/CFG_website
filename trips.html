The Isle of Arran

Arran is a small island, 20 miles long and 56 miles round, located off the south-west coast of Scotland.

Two ferries run from the Scottish mainland to the Isle of Arran. Most people reach the island via Ardrossan which has a direct rail service from Glasgow. These ferries are operated by CalMac.

You can hike up Goatfell, spend a day at the beach, play golf or visit Brodick Castle.

Loch Lomond

Loch Lomond is Britain's largest freshwater lake.

Located just 30miles North of Glasgow, the lake is really easy to reach.

You can get there by train, bus, car or even by bike.

Edinburgh

Edinburgh is Scotland's capital.

Hiking up Arthur's Seat, visiting the Castle, shopping or just sight-seeing- there is a lot to do in Edinburgh.

You can get to Edinburgh by train, bus or car.
